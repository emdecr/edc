GoStudy

GOStudy is an application that helps you find study spots + Spotify playlists that you can listen to while nerding out.

First, pick the desired noise level of your study environment (quiet = library, loud = cafe).

Next, pick how you want to get there. Choose 'walk' to find nearby places. Choose 'bike' or 'drive' if you want to find places that are further away from your regular area. A change of scenery might be a good idea!

Finally, pick a music type. Sometimes you need a good studying playlist. We can help you with that.

Now, GO STUDY! :)
